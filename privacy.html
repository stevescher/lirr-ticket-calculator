Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Overview

LIRR Ticket Calculator is a free, browser-based tool. It does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on any server. Everything happens locally in your browser.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information
  • Location data
  • Payment or financial information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Cookies of any kind

Local Storage

The app uses your browser's localStorage to save your calendar selections (which days you've toggled and their fare modes) between sessions. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is stored per month (e.g., lirr-2026-4)
  • Contains only day numbers and fare mode values — no personal information
  • Can be cleared at any time via your browser's settings (Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → Cached data and cookies)

Shareable Links

The "Copy Link" button creates a URL that encodes your selected days and fare modes in the URL hash (the part after #). For example: #202604:2,5,10.

This data is encoded in the URL only — it is not sent to any server by this app. However, anyone you share the link with will be able to see which days you selected. Only share links with people you're comfortable sharing that information with.

Hosting

This app is hosted on Vercel. As the hosting provider, Vercel may log standard web server data such as IP addresses, request timestamps, and browser user agents as part of normal infrastructure operations. This is governed by Vercel's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No external scripts are loaded.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since no information is collected from anyone, this applies equally to all users.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes materially (for example, if analytics are added), the "Last updated" date above will be updated. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
