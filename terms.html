About This Tool

LIRR Ticket Calculator is a free personal finance tool that helps commuters estimate and compare the cost of LIRR fare options for a given month. It is provided at no cost, with no registration required.

Fare Accuracy

Fare prices displayed in this app are based on publicly available MTA/LIRR data and are updated on a best-effort basis. Fares may not reflect current pricing. The MTA periodically adjusts fares without advance notice in this tool.

Always verify current fares directly with the MTA or LIRR before making purchasing decisions. The fare editor in the app allows you to update prices manually if they have changed.
